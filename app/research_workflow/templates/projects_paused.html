Why Pause Projects?
  • Waiting for Information: Key data or reports not yet available
  • Market Conditions: Waiting for better entry point or clarity
  • Time Constraints: Need to focus on higher priority research
  • Complexity: Need to build more knowledge before continuing
Best Practices
  • Review paused projects monthly to decide: resume, complete, or abandon
  • Add notes when pausing to remember why you paused
  • Set reminders for time-sensitive paused projects
  • Don't let projects stay paused for more than 3 months
