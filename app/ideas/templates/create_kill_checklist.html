📝 Writing Effective Kill Criteria
  • Make questions binary (clear yes/no answer)
  • Focus on dealbreakers, not nice-to-haves
  • Be specific with thresholds (e.g., "P/E < 30" not "reasonable valuation")
  • Order by importance - put the easiest/quickest to answer first
  • Include a mix of quantitative and qualitative criteria
