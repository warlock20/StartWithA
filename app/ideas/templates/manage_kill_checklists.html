💡 Suggested Kill Criteria to Get Started

Here are some proven kill criteria from successful investors:

  • "Can I understand this business in 5 minutes?" - Complexity is the enemy of execution
  • "Is the valuation reasonable (P/E < 30)?" - Price discipline prevents most mistakes
  • "Does management have significant skin in the game (>5% ownership)?" - Alignment matters
  • "Is this within my circle of competence?" - Stay in your lane
  • "Can I identify a sustainable competitive advantage?" - No moat = commodity business
  • "Is the balance sheet healthy (Debt/Equity < 0.5)?" - Leverage kills in downturns
  • "Would I be comfortable owning this for 10 years?" - Think long-term
