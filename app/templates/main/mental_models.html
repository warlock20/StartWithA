Mental Models for Investing

A collection of frameworks to structure your thinking, avoid biases, and build a durable investment thesis.

Checklist Analysis

Inspired by Charlie Munger

The foundation of disciplined research. Use a systematic, item-by-item evaluation to ensure you never miss a critical step in your due diligence. This model helps prevent errors of omission and combats emotional decision-making.

Go to Checklists

Destination Analysis

Inspired by Nick Sleep & Qais Zakaria

A long-term framework for portfolio companies. Instead of focusing on the next quarter, you set multi-year checkpoints for your thesis. This model forces you to think about a company's enduring competitive advantages and long-term trajectory.

Go to Companies

Digital Scuttlebutt

Inspired by Philip Fisher

A modern approach to "boots-on-the-ground" investigation. Aggregate recent news, press releases, and transcripts to gather qualitative insights and understand the current narrative around a company beyond the financial statements.
