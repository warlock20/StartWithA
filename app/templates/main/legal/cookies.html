Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are used to make websites function properly, provide security features, and remember your preferences.

Cookies We Use

We only use strictly necessary cookies that are essential for the Platform to function. We do not use any analytics, tracking, advertising, or third-party marketing cookies.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Provider
session Maintains your authenticated session after login. Contains an encrypted session identifier — no personal data is stored in the cookie itself. 1 hour (or until browser is closed) This Platform
remember_token Keeps you signed in across browser sessions when you select "Remember me" at login. Only set if you explicitly opt in. 30 days This Platform

Cookie Security Settings

  • Secure: Cookies are only transmitted over HTTPS
  • HttpOnly: Cookies cannot be accessed by JavaScript (prevents XSS attacks)
  • SameSite=Lax: Cookies are not sent with cross-site requests (prevents CSRF attacks)

Third-Party Authentication Cookies

When you log in via Auth0 (our authentication provider), Auth0 may set its own cookies on the auth0.com domain during the login process. These cookies are necessary for the authentication flow and are governed by Auth0's Privacy Policy.

Legal Basis

All cookies used by this Platform are strictly necessary for its operation. Under § 25(2) TDDDG (German Telecommunications Digital Services Data Protection Act) and Art. 5(3) of the ePrivacy Directive, strictly necessary cookies are exempt from the consent requirement.

This means we do not need to show a cookie consent banner for these cookies. However, we provide this policy for full transparency about what is stored on your device.

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling the session cookie will prevent you from logging in to the Platform.

For more information about cookies and how to manage them, visit allaboutcookies.org.

Further Information

For complete details about how we process your personal data, see our Privacy Policy.
