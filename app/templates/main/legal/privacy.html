2. Overview of Data Processing

This platform is an investment research tool that helps you organize and analyze your own investment research. We process the following categories of personal data:

  • Account data — name, email, profile picture (from your authentication provider)
  • Research data — companies, checklists, notes, journal entries, and analyses you create
  • AI interaction data — questions and answers exchanged with our AI research assistant
  • Usage data — preferences, settings, and platform interaction metadata
  • Technical data — session cookies, IP address (in server logs)

3. Account & Authentication Data

Authentication is handled by Auth0 (operated by Okta, Inc., USA). When you sign up or log in, the following data is transmitted from Auth0 to our platform:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture URL
  • Authentication provider (e.g., Google, GitHub, email/password)
  • Unique user identifier

Auth0's role: Auth0 acts as a data processor on our behalf. Auth0 may store your authentication credentials (e.g., hashed passwords) on their servers. Auth0 is certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework. For details, see Auth0's Privacy Policy.

Legal basis: Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) — processing is necessary to provide you with an account and access to the platform.

9. Your Rights (GDPR Art. 12-23)

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access (Art. 15) — You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16) — You can request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17) — You can request deletion of your account and all associated data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restriction (Art. 18) — You can request that we restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20) — You can request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format (JSON).
  • Right to object (Art. 21) — You can object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Right to withdraw consent (Art. 7(3)) — Where processing is based on consent (e.g., AI features), you can withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [your@email.com]. We will respond within 30 days as required by GDPR.

Right to lodge a complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority, in particular in the EU Member State of your habitual residence. The competent authority in Germany depends on your federal state. A list is available at BfDI — State Data Protection Authorities.

10. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained as long as your account is active. Deleted upon account deletion request.
  • Research data: Retained as long as your account is active. Fully deleted upon account deletion.
  • AI interaction logs: Retained for 90 days, then anonymized. Aggregated, non-personal metrics may be retained indefinitely for service improvement.
  • Server logs: Automatically deleted after 30 days.

11. Security Measures

We implement the following technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure, HttpOnly, SameSite session cookies
  • Authentication via Auth0 (industry-standard security)
  • Access restricted to authorized users only
  • Database access restricted to application layer only
  • API keys and credentials stored as environment variables, never in source code
