Getting Started with Sector Research

Welcome to your {{ analysis.sector_name }} research notebook! Here's how to conduct systematic sector analysis:

Understand the Framework
  • Sector Overview: Market size, growth rate, key segments
  • Industry Analysis: Porter's 5 Forces framework
  • Trends & Catalysts: What's changing and why
  • Risks & Challenges: What could go wrong
  • Investment Opportunities: Where to find alpha
Research Process
  • Click each section below to expand and start researching
  • Use the rich text editor to format notes, add links, create lists
  • Work is auto-saved as you type (no need to click save!)
  • Add sources in the sidebar to track your research materials
  • Build a question bank for evaluating companies in this sector
Example Research Questions

Overview:

  • What is the total addressable market?
  • What is the historical growth rate?
  • What are the key segments?

Competitive Dynamics:

  • How fragmented is the industry?
  • What are barriers to entry?
  • Who has pricing power?

Investment Thesis:

  • What secular tailwinds exist?
  • What makes a winner in this sector?
  • What metrics matter most?

Pro Tip: Thorough sector research upfront makes individual company analysis 10x faster and more insightful!
