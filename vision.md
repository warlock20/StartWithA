# Vision

A platform that improves the investment research process by connecting research, portfolio monitoring, and journaling into one disciplined workflow. The goal: help the investor follow a consistent process, learn from every decision, and stay focused — away from the daily noise.

## Three Parts

### 1. Research

The tools to find, evaluate, and decide on investment ideas.

- **Idea Inbox** — capture ideas quickly before they get lost
- **Kill Checklist** — screen out bad ideas early, before wasting time
- **Company Research** — structured deep-dive with templates and checklists
- **Sector Analysis** — understand industry context
- **AI Research Assistant** — document Q&A and analysis support

Flow: `Idea → Kill Screen → Research → Decision`

### 2. Portfolio

The tools to monitor positions and track whether reality matches the thesis.

- **Position Tracking** — transactions, cost basis, unrealized P&L
- **Thesis vs Reality** — does the investment thesis still hold?
- **Destination Checkpoints** — milestones to watch for each position
- **AI Analytics** — behavioral patterns, sector momentum, tax optimization

Flow: `Buy Decision → Track Position → Monitor Thesis → Review Checkpoints`

### 3. Journal

The tools to log decisions, capture mistakes, and build lasting insights.

- **Decision Journal** — record the reasoning behind every buy/sell
- **Mistake Log** — document what went wrong and why
- **Learning Insights** — patterns extracted from your own history

Flow: `Decision → Outcome → Reflect → Learn`

## The Connection

The three parts reinforce each other:

- Research quality feeds into portfolio outcomes
- Portfolio outcomes feed into journal reflections
- Journal insights improve future research

The platform tracks this loop, showing the investor where their process is strong and where it breaks down.
